Colophone is your ebook library made awesome: browse your tags nice and clean, show series stats, click and read your favourite books all in one place.

## Installation

Install from the [releases page](https://github.com/gennaro-tedesco/metabrowser/releases) choosing your architecture.

## Features

Watch the demo below and be amazed.

<img width="1687" height="1345" alt="Image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/ed2d8af3-0223-45b4-8478-4b1a8e4d211a" />

### Library view

Browse your library by tags, series, authors, language. Tap and toggle tag cards on and off the search bar and combine them to filter accordingly!

### Stats view

Visualise your library views interactively

### Reader

A reader with a beautiful interface to indulge yourself distraction-free, of course powered by vim keybindings (it goes without saying).

## Development

Build locally with

```sh
wails build && open /build/bin/colophon.app
```
